Adjective animals

Adjective animals is a game that makes you have to choices: pictures or descriptions.If you choose picture, then there will be some cut images of a specific animal shown to you. Then you will ave to guess the animal only by those pictures. If you choose description, then a description of an animal will appear on your screen and you will have to guess the animal correctly.

Snake

Snake - Snake is the clasic game of snake we all play or used to play as kids. The objective is to be able to eat as many fruits as possible without dying. You will have the body of a snake and you cant cross over yourself or touch the walls, or else you die. As you eat more and more fruits, the longer you get, and the harder the game gets.

Genius

Genius - Genius is another classic game that we used to play as kids. The old game is based on a screen which contains four colors. As you see the colors, they show a pattern and your objective is to repeat that exact same pattern, clicking the same colors as the pattern. After you reapeat the pattern, a new one will appear and you will have to do as many as you can. When you loose by clicking the wrong color in the pattern.
